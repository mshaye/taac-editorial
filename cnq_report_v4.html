Canadian Natural Resources Limited is one of the largest independent crude oil and natural gas producers in Canada. Operations are focused in the Western Canadian Sedimentary Basin, the U.K. North Sea, and Offshore West Africa.

CNQ's asset base includes Horizon Oil Sands (long-life, low-decline mining and upgrading), thermal in-situ operations, and a diversified portfolio of conventional light and heavy crude oil and natural gas assets. The company is characterized by strong free cash flow generation and a progressive dividend policy.
